# Phase 22 — UI Design Contract

> Visual and interaction contract for the two new onboarding wizard steps added in Phase 22:
> 1. Plan Selection Screen (`/onboarding/plan`) — Step 5 of 6
> 2. Post-Checkout Celebration Screen (`/onboarding/checkout-success`) — Step 6 of 6
>
> Generated by gsd-ui-researcher, verified by gsd-ui-checker.

---

## Scope

This contract covers ONLY the two new wizard steps inserted into the existing onboarding flow. No dashboard pages, no billing dashboard, and no paywall screen are in scope for Phase 22 (those belong to Phase 25).

**Onboarding wizard step count changes:** Layout currently declares `TOTAL_STEPS = 4`. Phase 22 extends this to `TOTAL_STEPS = 6` (adding Plan Selection as step 5 and Checkout Success as step 6). The layout's `getStep()` map must be updated to include `/onboarding/plan` → 5 and `/onboarding/checkout-success` → 6.

## Layout Contract

Both screens render inside the existing `OnboardingLayout` (`src/app/onboarding/layout.js`). They inherit:
- Outer background: `#F5F5F4` (warm stone) with `GridTexture variant="light"`
- Top orange radial gradient overlay (rgba(194,65,12,0.06))
- Max-width container: `max-w-lg mx-auto px-6 py-8 sm:py-12`
- Logo header + step counter in the top bar
- Orange progress bar at `--color-primary` (`#C2410C`)
- White wizard card: `bg-white rounded-2xl shadow-[0_1px_3px_0_rgba(0,0,0,0.04),0_6px_24px_-4px_rgba(0,0,0,0.06)] border border-stone-200/60 px-6 py-8 sm:px-8`
- Mobile: `max-sm:rounded-none max-sm:shadow-none max-sm:border-none max-sm:px-4 max-sm:py-6`

**Exception for Plan Selection:** The plan cards are wider than `max-w-lg`. The card grid must break out of the wizard card and render below it, OR the layout switches to `max-w-4xl` for step 5 only. Preferred approach: step 5 renders the plan grid **below** the wizard card header (outside the white card) in a `max-w-4xl` container centered in the same page. The wizard card holds only the heading + subheading; the plan cards sit beneath it in the same page column.

## Typography

Inherits Inter from the shared layout. Four roles for these two screens:

| Role | Size | Weight | Line Height | Usage |
|------|------|--------|-------------|-------|
| Body | 14px | 400 (regular) | 1.5 | Feature list items, card descriptions, subheading body |
| Label | 12px | 600 (semibold) | 1.4 | "Most Popular" badge, price unit `/mo`, trial note |
| Heading | 20px | 600 (semibold) | 1.2 | Wizard card step heading (e.g., "Choose your plan") |
| Display | 30px | 600 (semibold) | 1.1 | Price amount on each plan card |

Maximum 4 sizes. Maximum 2 declared weights (400 + 600). Label elements use semibold (600) for badge legibility and emphasis, consistent with the heading weight tier.

## Color

The Plan Selection screen renders plan cards in a **light context** (white wizard card, `#F5F5F4` background) — NOT the dark (`#1A1816`) context used on the public pricing page. The existing `PricingTiers.jsx` dark card styles must NOT be reused directly. A light-surface variant is required.

| Role | Value | Usage |
|------|-------|-------|
| Dominant (60%) | `#FFFFFF` / `#F5F5F4` | Wizard card background, page background |
| Secondary (30%) | `#F9F9F8` with `border border-stone-200/60` | Plan card surface (light variant) |
| Accent (10%) | `#C2410C` (orange-700) | Progress bar, selected plan ring, primary CTA button |
| Accent bright | `#F97316` (orange-500) | Check icons in feature list, "Most Popular" badge background |
| Destructive | N/A for this phase | No destructive actions in these two wizard steps |
| Success | `#166534` (green-800) | Celebration checkmark SVG stroke |
| Muted text | `#475569` | Descriptions, secondary copy, step counter |

**Accent reserved for:**
- Orange-700 (`#C2410C`): Progress bar fill, Growth plan card ring highlight (`ring-2 ring-[#C2410C]/40`), primary CTA button on Growth card
- Orange-500 (`#F97316`): Check icons in feature lists, "Most Popular" badge background, non-highlighted plan card CTA hover state

**Plan card light-surface specification:**
- Default card: `bg-white border border-stone-200/60 rounded-xl hover:border-[#C2410C]/30 hover:shadow-[0_0_20px_rgba(194,65,12,0.12)] transition-all duration-200`
- Growth (highlighted) card: `ring-2 ring-[#C2410C]/40` on top of default card styles
- Price color: `text-[#0F172A]` (slate-900)
- Feature text: `text-[#475569]` (slate-600)
- Tier name: `text-[#0F172A]` weight 600

## Screen 1: Plan Selection (`/onboarding/plan`)

### Route
`src/app/onboarding/plan/page.js` — Step 5 of 6

### Structure

```
OnboardingLayout (inherited — progress bar at 83%)
  Wizard card (max-w-lg)
    Heading: "Choose your plan"
    Subheading: "Your 14-day free trial starts today. No charges until your trial ends."
    Trial trust badge (inline, centered)
  Plan card grid (max-w-4xl, below wizard card)
    PlanSelectionCards (3 cards: Starter, Growth, Scale)
    Note below grid: "Need more? Contact us for Enterprise."
```

### Trial Trust Badge

Centered, pill-shaped badge rendered between the wizard card and plan grid:

```
● 14-Day Free Trial · Credit card required · Cancel anytime
```

- Container: `inline-flex items-center gap-2 px-4 py-2 rounded-full bg-[#FFF7ED] border border-[#FDBA74]/60 text-sm text-[#92400E]`
- Orange dot: `size-1.5 rounded-full bg-[#F97316]`

### Plan Cards (light variant)

3 cards in a responsive grid: `grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-3 gap-5`.

Growth card is visually elevated with:
- `ring-2 ring-[#C2410C]/40`
- "Most Popular" badge: `bg-[#C2410C] text-white text-xs font-semibold px-3 py-1 rounded-full`
- CTA button: `bg-[#C2410C] text-white hover:bg-[#C2410C]/90 shadow-[0_4px_16px_rgba(194,65,12,0.25)] w-full min-h-[44px] rounded-lg`

Starter and Scale CTA buttons: `bg-white border border-stone-300 text-[#0F172A] hover:bg-stone-50 w-full min-h-[44px] rounded-lg`

**No billing toggle on this screen.** Monthly pricing only. Annual pricing is display-only on the public page; at checkout time, only monthly Stripe prices are available in Phase 22. Do NOT include the annual/monthly toggle.

### Interaction States

| State | Behavior |
|-------|----------|
| Default | All 3 cards visible, Growth visually elevated |
| Card hover | `hover:-translate-y-0.5 hover:shadow-md` on non-highlighted cards |
| CTA click | Calls `POST /api/stripe/checkout-session` with `planId`, shows loading spinner on button, disables all CTA buttons while loading |
| Loading | Button shows `<Loader2 className="animate-spin size-4 mr-2" />` + "Redirecting..." label, `disabled` attribute on all 3 CTAs |
| API error | Inline error message below the grid: "Something went wrong. Please try again, or [contact support](/contact?type=support)." with a retry option (re-enables buttons) |
| Redirect | `window.location.href` to Stripe Checkout URL returned by API |

### Accessibility

- Plan card `<article role="article" aria-label="{TierName} plan — ${price}/month">`
- CTA button `aria-label="Start 14-day free trial on the {TierName} plan"`
- Loading state: `aria-busy="true"` on wrapper, `aria-disabled="true"` on all 3 buttons
- Error message: `role="alert"` for screen reader announcement

## Screen 2: Post-Checkout Celebration (`/onboarding/checkout-success`)

### Route
`src/app/onboarding/checkout-success/page.js` — Step 6 of 6

### Query Parameters
`?session_id={CHECKOUT_SESSION_ID}` — provided by Stripe success redirect. The page uses this to verify the session server-side before marking onboarding complete.

### Structure

```
OnboardingLayout (inherited — progress bar at 100%)
  Wizard card (max-w-lg)
    CelebrationOverlay (reused, adapted)
    Heading: "You're all set!"
    Subheading: "Your 14-day free trial is active. No charges until [trial_end_date]."
    Trial info row (plan name + trial end date)
    Primary CTA: "Go to Dashboard →"
    Auto-redirect notice: "Taking you to your dashboard in 5 seconds..."
```

### Celebration Overlay Adaptation

Reuse `CelebrationOverlay` component from `src/components/onboarding/CelebrationOverlay.js` without modification. The existing animated SVG checkmark (green `#166534` stroke, orange `#C2410C` pulse rings) matches the required success state.

### Trial Info Row

Displayed between subheading and CTA button:

```
[Plan badge]  [Trial end date]
Growth plan   Free until April 9, 2026
```

- Container: `flex items-center justify-center gap-4 mt-4 p-3 rounded-lg bg-[#F0FDF4] border border-[#BBF7D0]`
- Plan badge: `text-sm font-semibold text-[#166534]`
- Trial date: `text-sm text-[#166534]`
- Date format: "Free until [Month D, YYYY]" — computed from `trial_ends_at` returned by the server

### Loading/Error States

The page performs a server-side fetch to verify the Stripe session before displaying confirmation. During verification:

| State | UI |
|-------|----|
| Verifying (< 2s) | Spinner centered in wizard card, no heading visible yet |
| Verification success | Full celebration layout shown |
| Verification failed | Error message: "We couldn't confirm your subscription. If you were charged, please contact support." + link to `/contact?type=support` |

### Auto-Redirect Behavior

- After 5 seconds, auto-redirect to `/dashboard` via `setTimeout(() => router.push('/dashboard'), 5000)`
- "Go to Dashboard" button skips the countdown immediately
- Countdown text: `"Taking you to your dashboard in {n} seconds..."` — updates every second from 5 to 1
- `aria-live="polite"` on countdown element for screen reader announcement

### Accessibility

- `role="status"` on the success wizard card container after verification succeeds
- Heading: `<h1>` — first and only h1 on this page
- CTA button: `aria-label="Go to your dashboard now"`
- Countdown: `aria-live="polite" aria-atomic="true"`

## Animation Contract

Both screens are inside `OnboardingLayout` which already wraps children in `<AnimatedSection>`. No additional animation library imports needed beyond what is established.

| Animation | Element | Trigger | Duration |
|-----------|---------|---------|----------|
| Fade + slide up | Plan card grid | On mount | 300ms, staggered per card (80ms offset) |
| Draw-circle + draw-check | CelebrationOverlay checkmark | After verification success | 500ms + 300ms (existing globals.css keyframes) |
| Radial pulse rings | CelebrationOverlay | After verification success | 800ms (existing globals.css keyframes) |
| Countdown tick | Auto-redirect notice | Every 1 second | Instant text swap |

`prefers-reduced-motion`: Respected by CelebrationOverlay (existing code). Plan card stagger should also be suppressed — use `AnimatedStagger`/`AnimatedItem` pattern from `PricingTiers.jsx` which conditionally applies animation based on Framer Motion's `useReducedMotion` hook.
