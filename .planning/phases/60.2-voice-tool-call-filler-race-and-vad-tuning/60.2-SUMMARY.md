## Lessons captured

1. **UAT-surfaced structural defects must be remediated in-phase, not deferred.** A "passed" SUMMARY on top of a silent-no-op Fix H would have cemented broken code. Plan 05 reverted before writing SUMMARY — right call.
2. **`session.say()` on `AgentSession(llm=RealtimeModel)` in livekit-agents 1.5.1 is unusable without a TTS.** Document this prominently; do not reintroduce the pattern without attaching a TTS (voice mismatch tradeoff) or switching to a separate audio source.
3. **`allow_interruptions=False` is silently downgraded on RealtimeModel sessions with server-side turn detection** (`agent_activity.py:905-914`). The kwarg is intent-only; the real un-interruption lever for Gemini Live is server-VAD tuning (Fix G).
4. **Mocked tests can hide production-only failures.** `tests/test_filler_audio.py` mocked `context.session.say` and passed; production logs exposed the TTS-missing RuntimeError on first deploy. When a tool relies on session-level machinery (TTS, STT, audio source), integration testing against a real `AgentSession` shape matters.
