## Area 5: FeaturesCarousel-4 pillars

### Initial recommendation
Claude proposed: 24/7 Answer / Real-Time Booking / Speaks Your Trade / 70+ Languages.

### User asked: "do these pillars and structure give the highest conversion for my target audience of home service business owners?"

Claude re-audited honestly. Identified Pillar 4 (70+ Languages) as geographically-dependent — strong for SG + urban US markets, weak for broader US. Offered alternative: Automated Lead Recovery as Pillar 4 for tighter "zero lost leads" narrative.

### User clarified target: "mixed US sg"

Claude recommended US-biased pillar set with SG coverage via non-pillar placements:
- **Pillars (1-4):** 24/7 AI Answering → Real-Time Calendar Booking → Speaks Your Trade → Automated Lead Recovery
- **70+ Languages survives in 3 places:** hero micro-proof line, Pillar 3 body copy (Singlish + code-switching), FAQ entry

Rationale: Lead Recovery converts equally in both markets; Languages pillar-value varies by market. Placing Languages in 3 non-pillar spots gives SG visitors more exposure than a single pillar would.

### Tradeoff accepted
A Singapore contractor who scans only the 4 pillars (not hero or FAQ) might miss the language story. Accepted because most visitors read hero before pillars.

**User's choice:** Accepted Claude's recommendation. ("this works")

**Cuts (from original 9 carousel features):** Custom Pickup Rules (Stay-in-Control handles), Lead Capture & CRM (implied by Booking), Post-Call SMS (Stay-in-Control Card 3), Invoicing & Estimates (different product area), Call Analytics & Dashboard (dashboard product, not inbound), 70+ Languages (survives outside pillars).
