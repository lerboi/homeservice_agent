# Phase 02.1: Public Marketing Landing Page - Context

**Gathered:** 2026-03-20
**Status:** Ready for planning

<domain>
## Phase Boundary

A public-facing marketing landing page that explains the HomeService AI product and converts home service business owners into signups. No authentication required to view. Replaces the current placeholder `page.js`. This is a single-page marketing site, not a multi-page website.

</domain>

<decisions>
## Implementation Decisions

### Page Structure & Sections
- **Section order:** Hero → How it Works → Features Grid → Social Proof → Final CTA
- **Sticky nav:** Logo left, "Start My 5-Minute Setup" CTA button right, stays visible on scroll. Smooth-scroll anchor links to sections.
- **Footer:** Simple — logo, copyright, placeholder links for terms/privacy
- **No pricing section** — focus on getting them to try it, pricing discussed later

### Visual Style & Tone
- **Vibe:** Modern SaaS professionalism + warm, human-friendly touches. Not corporate, not generic.
- **Trade-inspired color palette:**
  - Midnight Slate `#0F172A` — primary (heavy-duty industrial feel)
  - Heritage Copper `#C2410C` — CTA buttons (physically connects to plumbing/HVAC trades)
  - Soft Stone `#F5F5F4` — warm earthy background
  - Muted Zinc `#475569` — secondary elements (iron/steel theme)
  - Evergreen `#166534` — success metrics and positive indicators
- **Imagery:** Product mockup/dashboard screenshot as hero visual + trade-specific illustration touches to warm it up
- **Motion:** Subtle scroll animations — sections fade/slide in on scroll (Framer Motion or CSS)

### Conversion & CTA Strategy
- **Primary CTA:** "Start My 5-Minute Setup" in Heritage Copper — links to `/onboarding` (existing wizard)
- **Secondary CTA:** "Watch Demo" in outline style — for visitors who need to see it first
- **3 CTA placements:** Hero (primary + secondary), after How it Works (primary), final section (primary)
- **No pricing on page** — conversion-focused, pricing comes after signup

### Content & Copy Direction
- **Headline approach:** Lead with missed calls = lost money pain point. Claude writes the final headline.
- **Copy tone:** Direct & confident ("you're losing jobs") + warm & empathetic ("we know you can't answer every call on a job site"). Short sentences, strong claims, relatable.
- **"The Foreman" voice** — copy speaks like a seasoned trades industry insider, not a SaaS marketer
- **Feature grid (4 boxes):**
  1. "The Night Shift, Sorted" — 24/7 AI answering
  2. "The Golden Lead Filter" — smart triage (emergency vs routine)
  3. "Money in the Calendar" — instant booking
  4. "Instant Emergency SMS" — priority alerts
  - Each box: one-sentence benefit + "Why it justifies $499/mo" line underneath
- **How it Works:** Plumber scenario — "Homeowner calls about a burst pipe at 11 PM → AI answers → triages as emergency → books next morning slot"
- **Social proof:** 3 placeholder testimonials from realistic trade personas (plumber, HVAC, electrician) with names/photos — replace with real ones later

### Claude's Discretion
- Final hero headline copy (guided by "missed calls = lost money" direction)
- Exact animation timing and easing
- Icon selection for feature grid (Lucide icons)
- Responsive breakpoints and mobile layout adjustments
- How it Works step count and visual treatment (3 vs 4 steps)
- Illustration style/approach for trade-specific touches
- Typography choices within the established palette
- "Watch Demo" destination (placeholder page or modal)

</decisions>
