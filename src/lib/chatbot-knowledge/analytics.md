# Analytics — Voco Dashboard

## What this section does
The Analytics section shows charts and metrics about your business performance. View revenue over time, track how many calls convert to leads and booked jobs, and see your lead pipeline broken down by status. Use date range filters to analyze specific periods.

## Key features
- **Revenue chart** — Bar chart of revenue by week or month
- **Lead conversion funnel** — Calls received → leads created → booked → completed
- **Pipeline donut chart** — Visual breakdown of current leads by status
- **Date range filter** — Analyze data for any custom date range
- **Key metrics** — Total calls, lead conversion rate, average job value

## How to navigate here
Go to [Analytics](/dashboard/more/analytics)

## Common tasks

### How do I see revenue for a specific period?
On the Analytics page, use the date range picker at the top to select a start and end date. The revenue chart and metrics update to show data for that period only.

### What is the lead conversion funnel?
The funnel shows how many calls resulted in leads, how many leads were booked as appointments, and how many were completed and paid. It helps you identify where leads are dropping off in the pipeline.

### How do I view my pipeline breakdown?
The donut chart shows your current leads grouped by status (New, Contacted, Booked, Completed, Paid). Hover over a segment to see the exact count and percentage.

### What does the conversion rate metric show?
The conversion rate shows what percentage of inbound calls resulted in a booked appointment. A higher rate means your AI receptionist and booking flow is working well.
