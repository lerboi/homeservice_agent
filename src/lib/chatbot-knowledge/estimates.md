# Estimates — Voco Dashboard

## What this section does
The Estimates section lets you create and send professional job estimates with good, better, and best pricing tiers. Customers can review the options and accept one. Once accepted, convert the estimate to an invoice with one click.

## Key features
- **Good/better/best tiers** — Present three pricing options so customers can choose their budget
- **Convert to invoice** — Turn an accepted estimate into an invoice instantly
- **Send to customers** — Send via email or SMS; customers view and accept online
- **Estimate statuses** — Draft, Sent, Accepted, Declined, Converted

## How to navigate here
Go to [Estimates](/dashboard/estimates)

## Common tasks

### How do I create an estimate?
Click "New Estimate" on the Estimates page. Add the customer's name and contact info. Create up to three pricing tiers (Good, Better, Best) by adding line items and descriptions for each option. Set the validity period and click "Save" or "Send."

### How do I send an estimate to a customer?
Open the estimate and click "Send Estimate." Enter or confirm the customer's email or phone number. The customer receives a link to review the pricing options and select one.

### How do I convert an estimate to an invoice?
Once a customer accepts an estimate, open the estimate and click "Convert to Invoice." The accepted tier's line items are copied to a new invoice automatically. You can edit the invoice before sending.

### What do the estimate statuses mean?
- **Draft** — Saved but not yet sent to the customer
- **Sent** — Delivered to the customer, awaiting response
- **Accepted** — Customer selected a pricing tier
- **Declined** — Customer declined the estimate
- **Converted** — Estimate has been turned into an invoice
