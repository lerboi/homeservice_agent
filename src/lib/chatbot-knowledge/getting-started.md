# Getting Started — Voco Dashboard

## What this section does
The dashboard home page gives you a quick snapshot of your business activity. It shows your most recent jobs, upcoming appointments, and a summary of recent call statistics. Use the setup checklist to get your account ready to start taking calls.

## Key features
- **Recent jobs** — See the latest jobs captured by your AI receptionist
- **Upcoming appointments** — View the next scheduled jobs on your calendar
- **Call stats** — Today's call count, answered vs missed ratio
- **Setup checklist** — Step-by-step guide to get your account fully configured
- **Take a tour** — Guided walkthrough of the dashboard features

## How to navigate here
Go to [Dashboard Home](/dashboard)

## Common tasks

### How do I complete my account setup?
Open the setup checklist on the dashboard home page. The key steps are: (1) connect your calendar (Google or Outlook) under Integrations, (2) configure your working hours under Settings, and (3) make a test call to verify your AI receptionist is working. Each step checks off automatically when complete.

### How do I take the guided tour?
Click the "Take a tour" button on the dashboard home page. The tour walks you through every major section of the dashboard with callouts and explanations.

### What do the home page cards show?
The cards show real-time data: recent jobs captured, upcoming appointments from your calendar, and call volume stats. These update automatically as your AI receptionist handles calls.
