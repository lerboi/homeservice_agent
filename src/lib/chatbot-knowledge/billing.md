# Billing — Voco Dashboard

## What this section does
The Billing section shows your current subscription plan, call usage for this billing period, and trial status if you are in trial. Upgrade your plan, manage your payment method, or view past invoices through the Stripe Customer Portal.

## Key features
- **Current plan** — Shows your plan name (Starter, Growth, or Scale) and call limit
- **Usage meter** — Shows how many calls you have used out of your plan limit this month
- **Trial countdown** — Shows days remaining in your free trial
- **Upgrade plan** — One-click to upgrade to a higher tier
- **Stripe Customer Portal** — Manage payment methods, download invoices, cancel subscription

## How to navigate here
Go to [Billing](/dashboard/more/billing)

## Common tasks

### How do I upgrade my plan?
On the Billing page, click "Upgrade Plan." You will see the available plans with their call limits and prices. Select your plan and complete payment through the secure Stripe checkout.

### How do I update my payment method?
Click "Manage Subscription" on the Billing page. This opens the Stripe Customer Portal where you can add or replace a credit card or bank account.

### How do I see my billing history and past invoices?
Click "Manage Subscription" to open the Stripe Customer Portal. Past invoices are listed there with download links for PDF receipts.

### What happens when I reach my call limit?
When you reach your monthly call limit, incoming calls will still be answered but you will receive a notification to upgrade. Review your usage on the Billing page to decide if you need a higher plan.

### How does the free trial work?
During your 14-day trial, you have full access to all features. Your card is charged only when the trial ends. The Billing page shows exactly how many days remain.
