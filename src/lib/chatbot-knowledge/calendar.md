# Calendar — Voco Dashboard

## What this section does
The Calendar section shows all your scheduled appointments booked by the AI receptionist. Connect your Google Calendar or Outlook to sync appointments two-way. Configure working hours so the AI only books jobs when you are available.

## Key features
- **Weekly and monthly view** — Switch between views using the toggle at the top
- **Appointment flyout** — Click any appointment to see caller details, job type, address, and notes
- **Calendar sync** — Two-way sync with Google Calendar or Outlook (connect under Integrations)
- **Working hours** — Set your available days and hours so the AI knows when to offer slots
- **Travel buffers** — Set travel time between appointments to avoid back-to-back bookings

## How to navigate here
Go to [Calendar](/dashboard/calendar)

## Common tasks

### How do I connect my Google Calendar?
Go to [Integrations](/dashboard/more/integrations) and click "Connect Google Calendar." Sign in with your Google account and grant calendar access. Appointments will sync automatically.

### How do I connect Outlook Calendar?
Go to [Integrations](/dashboard/more/integrations) and click "Connect Outlook Calendar." Sign in with your Microsoft account. Appointments sync both ways.

### How do I set my working hours?
Go to [Working Hours](/dashboard/more/working-hours) under Settings. Set your available days and time slots for each day. The AI will only offer appointment times within these hours.

### How do I set travel buffers between jobs?
Go to [Service Zones](/dashboard/more/service-zones) under Settings. You can set a travel buffer (in minutes) that the system adds between appointments to account for drive time.

### How do I view an appointment's details?
Click the appointment block on the calendar. A flyout opens with the caller's name, phone, job type, address, and any notes from the AI call.
