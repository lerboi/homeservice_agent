# Integrations — Voco Dashboard

## What this section does
The Integrations section lets you connect your external tools to Voco. Connect your Google Calendar or Outlook to sync appointments automatically. Connect QuickBooks or Xero to sync invoices and payments to your accounting software.

## Key features
- **Google Calendar** — Two-way sync: appointments booked by AI appear in Google Calendar and your Google Calendar events block Voco slots
- **Outlook Calendar** — Same two-way sync for Microsoft Outlook / Microsoft 365
- **QuickBooks** — Sync invoices and customer records to QuickBooks Online automatically
- **Xero** — Sync invoices to Xero for accounting

## How to navigate here
Go to [Integrations](/dashboard/more/integrations)

## Common tasks

### How do I connect Google Calendar?
Go to [Integrations](/dashboard/more/integrations) and click "Connect" next to Google Calendar. Sign in with your Google account and authorize calendar access. Existing appointments will sync and new bookings will appear in your Google Calendar automatically.

### How do I connect Outlook Calendar?
Click "Connect" next to Outlook Calendar on the Integrations page. Sign in with your Microsoft account. Calendar sync activates immediately after authorization.

### How do I connect QuickBooks?
Click "Connect" next to QuickBooks on the Integrations page. You will be redirected to QuickBooks to authorize the connection. Once connected, invoices you create in Voco will sync to QuickBooks automatically.

### How do I disconnect an integration?
Click "Disconnect" next to the connected integration. This stops the sync but does not delete any data already synced. You can reconnect at any time.

### Why is my calendar not syncing?
Check the Integrations page to confirm the connection is active (not showing "Reconnect" or an error). If the connection expired, click Reconnect and re-authorize. Calendar tokens can expire if you change your password or revoke access.
