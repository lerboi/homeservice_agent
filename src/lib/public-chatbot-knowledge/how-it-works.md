# How Voco Works

## 5-Minute Setup

1. **Enter your business name and trade** — plumbing, HVAC, electrical, roofing, handyman, etc.
2. **Add your contact details** — phone number and notification preferences
3. **Hear your AI receptionist** — make a test call to hear how it sounds before going live
4. **Choose your plan** — Starter, Growth, or Scale (14-day free trial included)
5. **Go live** — your AI receptionist starts answering calls immediately

No technical skills needed. No IT team. If you can fill out a form on your phone, you can set up Voco.

## What Happens on a Call

1. **Answers in under 2 rings** with your business name and a professional tone
2. **Captures caller details** — name, phone number, address, job type
3. **Triages urgency** — distinguishes emergencies (burst pipe, no heat) from routine requests
4. **Books the appointment** — checks your calendar availability and confirms the slot
5. **Sends you a summary** — SMS notification with all details the moment the call ends

## What If the AI Gets Confused?

If a caller asks something outside the AI's scope, it captures their info and routes the call to you with a full summary. You never lose a lead — even on edge cases.

## Can Callers Tell It's AI?

Some might sense it, but most don't stop to ask. What matters is that your phone gets answered in one ring, every time, with your business name and a professional tone. Callers care more about being helped than who's helping.
