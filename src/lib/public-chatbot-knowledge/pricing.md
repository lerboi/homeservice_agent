# Voco Pricing

## Plans

All paid plans include the same full feature set. The only difference is call volume and support level.

### Starter — $99/month
- 40 calls/month (480/year)
- Overage: $2.48/call
- Email support
- Best for: Solo operators just getting started

### Growth — $249/month (Most Popular)
- 120 calls/month (1,440/year)
- Overage: $2.08/call
- Priority email support
- Best for: Growing crews booking 3–5 jobs per day

### Scale — $599/month
- 400 calls/month (4,800/year)
- Overage: $1.50/call
- Priority support + onboarding call
- Best for: Multi-crew operations at full capacity

### Enterprise — Custom pricing
- Unlimited calls
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom integrations and SLAs
- Best for: Franchises and multi-location operations
- [Contact Us](/contact?type=sales) for pricing

## Annual Discount

Save 20% with annual billing. Annual prices: Starter $79/mo, Growth $199/mo, Scale $479/mo.

## 14-Day Free Trial

Sign up, complete the 5-minute setup, and your AI receptionist starts answering calls immediately. Full 14 days to evaluate with real calls.

## Overage Policy

We don't cut you off mid-month. Calls beyond your plan limit are charged at a transparent per-call rate shown on your invoice. Only calls lasting 20+ seconds count — quick hang-ups and wrong numbers are always free.

## Upgrading Mid-Cycle

You only pay the difference. If you're on Starter and upgrade to Growth halfway through, we prorate the cost — you're only charged for the remaining time at the new rate.

## Cancellation

No lock-in contracts. Cancel from your dashboard settings at any time. Service stops at the end of the current billing period. No cancellation fees.
